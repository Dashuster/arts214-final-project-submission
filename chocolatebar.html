Packaging for a chocolate brand. Placeholder text.Logo design was created for a young, small and promising vegetarian cafe in Williamsburg.

All-vegan menu is based on veggies and grains. Kale, as the new king of greens, is one of the top ingredients used. The name of the cafe is based on the old Scottish word meaning vegetable garden (literally cabbage garden). Owners, young enthusiastic couple, wanted something simple, modern and appealing to the Williamsburg vegan foodies.
