SIGGI'S


Siggi’s is a young growing company created by a homesick Iceland native who was left alone in NYC on Christmas Holiday. Skyr yoghurt is a natural product that, unlike usual yoghurt, contains more protein then sugar.

The unusual flavors were the main focus and the inspiration for this design as it’s not something you’re used to see on the store shelves.

The result is a funny colorful and intriguing ad.
