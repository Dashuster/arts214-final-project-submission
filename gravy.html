DASHA SHUSTER

JERSEY ITALIAN GRAVY


The Original Jersey Italian Gravy is a NYC company that creates an authentic Italian taste in a small Brooklyn facility.

One of the comment on Amazon became my inspiration. It reminded the reviewer of the gravy made by his old Italian Great Grandma.

The idea behind the design is that in addition to cooking amazing meals most grandmas have one thing in common - they are great sewers. I wanted my design to bring back childhood memories that would make people want to buy the product (or at least to call their grandma).

The work was selected as one of the top design in City Tech and submitted for official contest.
